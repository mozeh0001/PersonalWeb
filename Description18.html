Spring 2024: Roblox x Open Alpha Game Jam

Office Oddity

  • Theme: “Wish”
  • Team: Five programmers
  • Timeframe: Two days to develop a Roblox game
  • Storyline: - A character thinks their colleagues forgot their birthday - They are surprised with a celebration at work - Mystery builds as the game unfolds - The player makes a wish by blowing out birthday candles
  • Project: Progressive story-driven game
  • Planning: Created a detailed storyboard before development
  • Benefits of Pre-Planning:
    • Outlined key narrative beats
    • Defined character interactions
    • Mapped gameplay mechanics
  • Storyboard Role: Served as a visual guide to refine ideas before implementation
  • Outcome: Ensured a cohesive and immersive player experience
