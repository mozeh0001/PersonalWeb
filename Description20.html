Fall 2022 Game Jam

Boo Who?

Play the game here!



  • Theme: “Mystery”
  • Team Size: 8 members with diverse skills in art, design/narrative, and programming
  • Game Concept:
    • Set at a Halloween party (October theme)
    • Players must guess which party guests are their friends in disguise
    • Each character’s costume reflects a distinct personality
  • Design Process: Created a character mood board to guide costume and personality design
  • Team Workflow: Formed specialized groups by expertise but shifted roles as needed
  • Development: Completed during an intense two-day game jam
