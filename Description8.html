Iron Testament

Programmer & Audio Designer

Unity | C# | GarageBand | Wwise | Blender

Iron Testament is a turn-based strategy game developed by Rat Economy, a team of 19 USC Games students, and showcased at the USC Games Expo. Players lead a sect of life-worshipping robots against an adversary AI uprising, using positioning and unique abilities to outmaneuver enemies through tactical, turn-based combat.

Transparency Trigger Based on Raycast

  • Implemented a transparency effect for objects obstructing the camera’s line of sight to a unit.
  • Used raycasts to detect intersections between the camera and objects.
  • Optimized the effect to only activate when the camera is close, accounting for raycast range limitations.


Tile Indicators

  • Created attack and range patterns around the bot grid.
  • Implemented Manhattan and Euclidean tile highlighting to represent ability ranges.
  • Enabled players to visualize how far their abilities could reach through grid highlights.

Programming the UI

  • Developed health bars that billboard to the camera.
  • Implemented a cyan preview for proposed health when damage is dealt.
  • Added lerping health indicators above bots for actual damage.
  • Built an in-game console system to log messages, similar to Minecraft or Call of Duty.
  • Enabled scrolling, fade-out, and timed removal of log messages.
  • Added toggle functionality between recent logs and full history.
  • Ensured scroll-to-bottom behavior for continuous updates.
  • Styled logs and text with Unity Rich-Text for color effects.
